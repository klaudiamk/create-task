About Us

Why Do We Do It?

Many people have dietary restrictions. They can have nut or dairy allergies or they can have lactose or gluten intolerance. There are many foods that people cannot eat certain foods because of this. kloud kafe offers baked goods that do not contain any nuts gluten, or dairy. This bakery cafe allows for anyone, including people who have dietary restrictions, to have a sweet treat.

Mission Statement

kloud kafe’s mission is to provide customers with delicious, healthy, and fresh café goods at fast service, along with the option of weekly art sessions to help relax oneself.

Vision Statement

kloud kafe hopes to achieve customer satisfaction through the use healthy, fresh ingredients and enjoyable experiences.
